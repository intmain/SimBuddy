Features

Everything you need, in your menubar

Simulator List

See all your simulators at a glance. Booted devices are highlighted at the top, shutdown ones are tucked away in a submenu.

Sandbox Explorer

Click any app to open its sandbox directory in Finder. Hold Option (⌥) to copy the path to clipboard instead.

Storage Manager

Find and clean dead folders, unused simulators, and bloated app sandboxes. Reclaim disk space with a few clicks.

Provisioning Profiles

One click to open the provisioning profiles directory in Finder. No more remembering the path.

Instant Access

Always in your menubar, always ready. Menu opens in under 400ms with cached data and background refresh.

Safe Deletion

All deletions go to Trash, not permanent delete. Confirmation dialogs show exactly what will be removed.

How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Install

Download the DMG, drag SimBuddy to Applications. It lives in your menubar — no dock icon.

2

Click

Click the menubar icon to see your simulators. Hover to browse installed apps.

3

Navigate

Click an app to jump straight into its sandbox in Finder. Option-click to copy the path.
